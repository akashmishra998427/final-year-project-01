About VCQRU Family

Welcome to VCQRU, your trusted partner for protecting your brand from the risks of counterfeiting and duplication. As an IT organization with vast experience and advanced industry knowledge, we specialize in providing cutting-edge anti-counterfeit solutions that ensure your products remain authentic and original. We are a group of young professionals with a distinct purpose and goals. Our company strongly values quality standards and we believe that adhering to these contributes significantly to our overall success.

The problem of counterfeiting is prevalent in a variety of industries such as FMCG, cosmetics, auto parts, health supplements, and consumer electronics. Counterfeit products are produced using unauthorized similar or low-quality components which deceive customers and create a threat to public safety. VCQRU offers an innovative and highly effective anti counterfeit solution to address this problem.

Our solution employs cutting-edge technology, like QR codes, holograms, and secure packaging, to guarantee the authenticity and originality of your products. With our system incorporated into your products and packaging, you can safeguard your brand against the dangers of replication and counterfeiting, assuring your customers' confidence.

Moreover, our assistance extends beyond that. We offer loyalty programs, brand protection, electronic warranties, digital marketing, website design and mobile application development. Our mission is to assist you in expanding your enterprise and accomplishing unparalleled achievements.
